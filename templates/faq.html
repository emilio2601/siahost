Why do I have to wait for my download to process?

The Sia renter currently does not support streaming downloads, so you'll have to wait for the server to assemble all the pieces until you are able to start your download. However, once the process is finished, the server keeps the file in cache for 7 days, so that the following downloads don't have a long lead time.

Is my data safe here?

In a nutshell, yes. It's stored redundantly (3x) around the world. But this is only a Proof-of-Concept, so probably this service won't stay forever. Or maybe it will. Don't count on it.

Is this service free?

Currently yes, because we're in the PoC stage. But eventually you'll be need to pay for storage and ingress/egress loading Siacoins into your account. Also, sometime in the future, we may support paying with fiat currency.
